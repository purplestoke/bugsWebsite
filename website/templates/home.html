The Hub of Competitive Gaming

Compete in events under a community banner

Compete with Ethereum for Ethereum

Anybody has a chance to win, but performance affects chance

After every bugs Event 10% of competitors selected split 60% of all Ethereum generated from Event Entry Cost Fees.

20% of Ethereum generated goes to a lucky winner from the community with the highest average score.

10% of Event Entry Cost Fees are given to participating communitys (per competitor)

The last 10% is retained by bugs.

What we do

bugs is a brand for content creators, streamers and communities. We give these communities the option to participate in bugs Events using the Discord bot, Stoke.

Those who invite the Stoke bot into their server give their community members the option to compete in a bugs event, where they not only compete for themselves but for their community as well.

All you need to compete in a bugs Event is a Discord account and Ethereum wallet.

Note* bugs DOES NOT support hosted wallets
